⚠️ First time opening VibeRadar?

  1. Open the downloaded VibeRadar.dmg and drag the app to your Applications folder.
  2. Double-click VibeRadar to launch it. macOS will show a warning saying the app is from an unidentified developer — this is normal for apps distributed outside the App Store.
  3. Open System Settings → Privacy & Security, then scroll down. You will see a message about VibeRadar being blocked.
  4. Click “Open Anyway” and confirm when prompted. You only need to do this once — after that, VibeRadar will open normally every time.
